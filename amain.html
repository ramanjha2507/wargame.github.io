About Game

Instructions: Defend the Seahorse


Goal:
 - Your mission is to control the seahorse and protect it from the incoming enemy fish.
Shoot fireballs at the enemy fish to earn points and prevent them from
reaching the seahorse.

Controls:
 - Use the UP and DOWN arrow keys to move the seahorse up and down along the vertical axis.

Shooting Fireballs:
 - Press the SPACEBAR to shoot fireballs towards the enemy fish.
- Aim accurately to hit the enemy fish and earn points for each successful hit.

Scoring:
 - You will earn points each time a fireball successfully hits an enemy fish.
- However, be cautious, if an enemy fish collides with the seahorse, your score will decrease.
- The game will display your current score throughout the gameplay.

Game Over:
 - The game ends when the seahorse's score reaches zero or falls below zero due to enemy collisions.
- Try to achieve the highest score possible by shooting down as many enemy fish as
you can without letting them touch the seahorse.

Restart:
 - If you wish to play again after the game is over, simply refresh the page to restart the game.

Good luck!
 - Show off your skills and protect the seahorse from the relentless enemy fish in this
thrilling space shoot game. Have fun and aim for the top score!
